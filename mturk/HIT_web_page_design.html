Instructions

CommonGen is a set of tasks for which AI models need to generate a sentence using a set of words provided, and it is allowed to use different tenses or forms of the given words. Sometimes the generated sentences from AI model may not cover all the given words. We would like you to assess how fluent, how sensible and how complex are the generations.

  1. How fluent are the generated sentences? Here, being fluent means that the generated sentence sounds natural to native speakers. Things that contribute to fluency include but not limited to grammatical structure, word and phrase usage, and punctuations. Note that the semantics of the sentence of the sentences is not NOT evaluated for fluency here! For example, you should NOT consider if a sentence is logical or plausible when scoring for fluency. Please rate the generated sentences on the scale from 1 to 5 with 1 being completely unreadable and 5 being sentences that native speakers may say in daily life.
  2. How sensible are the generated sentences? Being sensible means that the generated sentence makes sense to normal human beings living in modern society. Things that contribute to the level of sense making include but not limited to the logics of the sentences, the plausibility of the sentences and the possibility of the event described by the sentence. Please rate the generated sentences on the scale from 1 to 5 with 1 being sentences that are completely unfathomable or sentences describing impossible events and 5 being perfectly logical statements or possible daily events.
  3. How complex are the generated sentences? Being complex means that the sentence contains much information and sophisticated structure. Things that contribute to the level of complexity include but not limited to the number of modifiers and clauses used in the sentence. Please rate the generated sentences on the scale from 1 to 5 with 1 being sentences containing only simple subjects, verbs and objects and 5 being sentences containing sophisticated structures, modifiers, clauses and much information.
Please select the appropriate answer choice in the below task.
